Our Products

Wan's Milktea offers a variety of rich, refreshing, and creamy milk teas made with high-quality ingredients. From classic favorites to unique flavors like Okinawa, Wintermelon, and Chocolate, each cup is customizable with a range of toppings, including tapioca pearls, jellies, and more. Perfectly crafted for every milk tea lover!

Okinawa

Rich and creamy with a deep caramel sweetness from Okinawa brown sugar.

Panda Pearl

Fun and chewy, featuring black-and-white panda pearls in a sweet milk tea base.

Wantermelon

Refreshing and fruity, combining watermelon sweetness with creamy milk tea for a light, tropical treat.

Chocolate

Decadent and indulgent, blending creamy milk tea with smooth chocolate.
